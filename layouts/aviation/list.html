§ 02 / Journey

Started with the Air Scouts and back to flying again after 20 years.

My aviation journey.

I first went gliding at 14 with the Air Scouts then life got in the way for two decades. Returning in 2022, I joined South Wales Gliding Club, went solo, got my SPL, achieved the Silver C badge and 100km diploma, and bought my own glider.
